Martin's Army Letters and Army Documents

Here are all 320 letters Martin wrote home while he served in England from 1943 to 1946 (plus 24 letters from other people). His mom saved every one!

I'm transcribing the letters to make them easier to read, adding photos and other material. This project is about half done but you can see my work so far!

You can also find some other interesting documents from Martin's Army years.
